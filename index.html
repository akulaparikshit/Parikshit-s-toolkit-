Advanced Statistical Testing Suite

Automatic Test Recommendation Engine
Based on normality results automatically recommend: One Sample T-Test, Independent T-Test, Paired T-Test, ANOVA, Mann-Whitney U, Wilcoxon Signed Rank, Kruskal-Wallis, or Chi-Square.
One Sample T-Test
Inputs: Numeric Column, Hypothesized Mean (μ₀). Outputs: Sample Mean, t Statistic, p Value, Decision. Visualizations: Bell Curve + Critical Region.
Paired T-Test
Inputs: Column A, Column B. Outputs: Mean Difference, t Statistic, p Value. Visualizations: Before/After Boxplot and Difference Histogram.
ANOVA
Inputs: Numeric Column + Group Column. Outputs: F Statistic, p Value, Decision. Visualizations: Group Boxplots and Mean Comparison Chart.
Chi-Square Test
Inputs: Two Categorical Variables. Outputs: χ² Statistic, Degrees of Freedom, p Value. Visualizations: Contingency Table and Frequency Heatmap.
